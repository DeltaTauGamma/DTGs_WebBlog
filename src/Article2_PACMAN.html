PACMAN!

I am making a replica of PACMAN and I am going to call it red-pacman. Why? Well...

Pacman is undoubtedly one of the most famous videogame of all time. Personally, I've been able to enjoy it a couple of times, however, that is not the reason why I'm making the replica. The actual reason is a meme I saw a while ago. To give you some context, in Spain pacman was advertised as "Comecocos" (somewhat translated to mind eater, kinda), so what this meme was doing was swapping the "cocos" for "rojos", ending up with "comerojos" which once translated means Communist eater ("Redeater"). This came up as really funny to me, so I decided to make a replica of pacman, with all the ghosts being different communist leaders or ideologists.

At this point you should know that the project is already a several months old, although I haven't worked on it for a very long time. As such, I'm not going to go through every step of the way, instead I'll simply show you how it is currently and then, once I have finished doing that, continue with development. So without further ado, let's begin!

I decided to do this game on Godot, an open-source and free game engine. I chose Godot for two reasons: the first is that I'm into open source and the second is that I really didn't feel like signing up for anything. In godot, I created the my node framework and added the tylemap.
